A SHORT BIOGRAPHY OF LATA MANGESHKAR:



  • Lata Mangeshkar was born on 28 sep 1929
  • Other names given to her was "queen of melody","nightingale of india","voice of millennium"
  • She was a playback singer, music composer film producer and many more
  • She was awarded by many awards like "national filmfare awards","BFJA awards","Filmare lifetime achievement award"
  • She was Honoured by "Padma bhushan", "Dadasaheb Phalke Award","Bharat Ratna" and many more
  • Lata ji pushed back the boundaries of music with her mind-boggling skills
  • She was suffering from multiple organ dysfunction syndrome
  • Lata Mangeshkar passed away on feb 6,2022
  • She will be loved and remembered by all of us
